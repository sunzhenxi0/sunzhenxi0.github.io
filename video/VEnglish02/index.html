Learn Faster by Reading ACTIVELY Does reading feel useless for you? I’ll show you how to fix that. Learn the difference between active and passive reading and discover practical techniques that help you remember more, understand faster, and actually learn from what you read. I explain why simply recognising words is not learning, and how encoding information makes it stick in your memory. You’ll learn how to immerse yourself in a text, forget what you think you already know, and start thinking the way the author thinks. I also cover practical habits like writing in books, asking your own questions, using KWL before reading, and choosing both fiction and non-fiction to develop emotional and logical understanding. These techniques turn reading into one of the most powerful tools you have for learning English (or anything else) faster.
