Say Sorry the Right Way: How to Make a Real Apology
Do you know the difference between saying sorry, excuse me, I regret, and I apologize? Many English learners get these phrases wrong. In this lesson, I’ll teach you different ways to apologize in different context, so you can use the right one at the right time – in the right way! We’ll talk about how to say “sorry” in English for small mistakes, how to use “excuse me” for polite interruptions or to get attention, when to use “I apologize” in formal situations, and cover other useful phrases like “I regret”, “that was my fault”, and “my bad”. I’ll also share my secret 4-step formula for a real apology!
